About Me

Hello, I'm Owen

I am a Computer Science and Business student at the University of Colorado Boulder with a passion for technology, leadership, and entrepreneurship. My dual degree program provides me with a unique perspective on applying technical solutions to business problems.

My academic journey bridges the gap between cutting-edge technology and business strategy, allowing me to understand both the technical implementation and strategic considerations of technology solutions.

Outside of academics, I'm deeply involved in campus leadership, particularly through my roles with the Interfraternity Council and Delta Tau Delta Fraternity. These experiences have sharpened my communication, problem-solving, and team management skills.

My technical interests include iOS development, data analysis, and innovative technology applications that make a positive impact. I'm constantly seeking opportunities to apply my combined knowledge of computer science and business to create meaningful solutions.
